The War for Ridgeback

Overview

Growing up, Jason Hornehill, lord to-be of Ashvale felt disdain towards the other lords of the land. They jostled for power, and were dismissive of Leyla Hornehill, Jason's mother. The fighting continued until Jason became lord, and he seized upon the opportunity to teach these other lords a lesson.
