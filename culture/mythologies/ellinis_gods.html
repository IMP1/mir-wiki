Ellinis Gods

Overview

The Ellinis Gods are part of an ancient pantheon. Its believers are found Midnighters, and specifically Midnighters from Ridgeback.

There are many gods, found in different aspects of nature.

Tenets and Beliefs

The foundational belief is that all aspects of the world are imbued with energy and thought. There are numerous gods, each representing an aspect of the world.
