Fauna of Mir

Sapient Species

Capable of thought and complex relationships, these animals can communicate both concrete and abstract ideas, and reason about the future and past.

  • Dragonborn
  • Dwarves
  • Elves
  • Humans
  • Cephalopods
  • Ursalba

Non-Sapient Species

Incapable of higher thought, these animals are domesticated, hunted, left alone, or deterred. They make up the bulk of the ecosystem of Mir.

Semi-Sapient Species

Capable of thought, but still considered uncivilised, these animals are considered dangerous and are often driven away from civilsation.
