Elves

Overview

Elves have spread themselves across Mir, but collect in areas where they can find the energies they need to recharge. Elves do this through connections to certain elements, instead of sleep. Some elves live in forests, needing connection to the energy of the trees. Some elves prefer the energy of certain metals and live in cities and are drawn to finery. Elves can see ultraviolet light, through their cones, and as such have a higher band of visible colours than other species. They also can see decently in low light, but only in greyscale, not seeing colours (including UV).
