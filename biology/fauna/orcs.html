Orcs

Overview

Orcs can see infrared (heat) radiation, but can't see very well towards the blue end of the spectrum. The sky and sea (and other blue objects) look very colourless, and a little dark, and they have an instinctual, unconscious fear of the ocean. They have little saturation in their skin, greyer than other creatures.
