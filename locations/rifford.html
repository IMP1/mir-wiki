Rifford

Overview

Rifford is a town in the north of the Ashvale region built on a crossroads, with main roads going to Ashvale to the south, the Thorgrund Pass to the north-west, and less-used road to Proudwatch to the east.

Name Origin

Deriving from River Ford, as Rifford was built around a river crossing point.
