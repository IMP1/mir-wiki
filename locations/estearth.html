Estearth

Overview

Estearth was subsumed into Ashvale in the War for Ridgeback. It remains sparsely inhabited, as its capital, Proudwatch (then First Morn) was destroyed.

People still refer to the lands as Estearth, but it is owned and managed by the Lord of Ashvale, who is also the King of Ridgeback.
