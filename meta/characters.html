Timeline

  1. early humans evolve
  2. some have higher oxygen sensitivity and move higher and higher up into mountains
  3. some have more sensitivity to sun and gradually spend more time below ground
  4. some have more sensitivity to magical energies and move to areas where they can recharge
  5. some fight/breed in the grasslands and forests and plains and a victor emerges
  6. as time went on, each of these four main branches spread and start to find each other
  7. meanwhile, underwater, stranger life was emerging
  8. also meanwhile, on other continents, other life (most of which non-sapient) was plodding along

Resultant Races

  • dragonborn (moved to high climate; adapted to life there; evolved in isolation)
  • delvers (moved underground; adapted to life there; evolved in isolation)
  • midnighters (mutated an attunement to magical energy. this gave them the advantage of higher natural magical gift, and not needing sleep in the traditional sense)
  • plainswalkers (humans)
  • cephalopod (octopuses, living in the sea)
  • panserbjorne (bears, living in the northern regions of the large icy continent)
